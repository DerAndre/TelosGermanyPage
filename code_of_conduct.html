Code of Conduct

Commitment

TelosGermany commits to having ongoing discussions with all token holders within all our communication channels, as well as designated community communication channels. We do our best to to form consensus within the token holder community by listen to all token holders opinions.

Constitution

TelosGermany will support and defend the guidelines and governance set forth by the EOS.IO Constitution. In order to protect the network we will not comply with any requests that are forbidden by the Constitution. We try to ensure the constitution is transparently communicated to all token holders.

Arbitration Decisions

TelosGermany will adhere to and support any arbitration ruling made by an appointed arbitrator. We will do all means necessary to ensure the ruling is carried out.

Ethical Standard

We declare we will always act with honesty and integrity while applying the highest ethical standards in all aspects of our conduct. This includes abiding by the EOS.IO Constitution and Block Producer Agreement.

Network Infrastructure

Our main priority is to support and maintain the network infrastructure stability and growth as the Telos network scales. Therefore we're reinvesting the revenues made. Our infrastructure security will be taken extremely serious, we will conduct third party audits to provide absolute transparency to the community.

Financial Autonomy

TelosGermany is funded and bootstrapped immediately by the founding team’s personal capital. It will disclose any investment into the company in complete transparency. TelosGermany will not allow any investor to have financial or operational control. TelosGermany will never own a percentage of another Block Producer Company.

Collaboration and Community

We believe it is a combined effort of the entire community to effectively run and govern the Telos Blockchain. TelosGermany participates in open discussions with the community to contribute to any current technical or governance issues. With the decentralised and open source nature of Telos, based on EOS.IO, the role of a block producer is to lead from the front, nurturing an environment where collaboration is encouraged.

Against Collusion

TelosGermany will do all means possible to prevent collusion with other block producers and make any partnerships completely transparent to the community.

Amendments to our Code of Conduct

Amendments to our Code of Conduct will be informed by the community’s will and will be broadcast to community members through all viable communication channels before formal adoption.
